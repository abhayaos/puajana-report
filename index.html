Restriction Report

Official Guidelines & Usage Limitations

Introduction

This restriction report defines the rules and limitations applied to ensure safety, discipline, and proper usage of the system.

Purpose of Restrictions

  • Prevent misuse of resources
  • Maintain security and privacy
  • Ensure controlled access

Scope

These restrictions apply to all registered users, administrators, and staff members accessing the system.

List of Restrictions

  1. Unauthorized access is strictly prohibited
  2. System files must not be modified
  3. External devices require prior approval
  4. Internet usage is limited to approved purposes
  5. Data sharing without permission is not allowed

Duration

These restrictions are effective immediately and remain active until further notice.

Consequences of Violation

  • Written warning
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent access termination

Authority

Issued and enforced by the System Administration Department.
